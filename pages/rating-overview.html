Categories

Scariness

The game's ability to invoke fear and terror in players through its atmosphere, encounters, and horror elements. (19%)

Sound Design

The quality and effectiveness of the game's audio elements, including music, sound effects, and voice acting, in creating an immersive and chilling experience. (12%)

Story

The narrative and plot of the game, including its depth, coherence, and ability to immerse. (10%)

Visuals

The graphical presentation and quality of the game, including models, environments, and visual effects. (10%)

Ambience

The overall mood and environment created by the game, including lighting, level design, and other atmospheric factors. (12%)

Gameplay

The mechanics, controls, and overall gameplay experience offered by the game. (19%)

Creativity

The originality and innovation of the game's concepts, mechanics, and design within the horror genre. (7%)

Enjoyment

The level of entertainment experienced while playing the game. (4%)

Production Quality

The overall craftsmanship and polish of the game. (4%)

Technical

The technical aspects of the game, such as loading times, stability, and performance. (3%)
